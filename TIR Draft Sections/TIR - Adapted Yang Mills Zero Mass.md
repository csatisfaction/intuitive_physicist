$$L = \Gamma\_{\text{int}} \cdot v \cdot r = n\hbar\_{N-1} \quad (\text{where } n \in \mathbb{Z}^+)$$

$$L = v^3 \cdot r^2 = n\hbar\_{N-1}$$

## 2.3 Velocity Saturation and the Sub-Quantum Energy Floor

We now introduce the limit condition of **velocity saturation**. $$v = c\_{N-1}, \quad n = 1$$

$$c\_{N-1}^3 \cdot r\_{\text{vacuum}}^2 = 1\hbar\_{N-1}$$

$$r\_{\text{vacuum}} = \sqrt{\frac{\hbar\_{N-1}}{c\_{N-1}^3}}$$
